Take a moment to stargaze

A ringed planet you steer with your hands. With the camera on, move a hand to orbit it, spread two hands to zoom, and pinch to open a moon — each holds a small note on mindfulness, psychology, and support, alongside your own journal entries. No camera? Your cursor (and the scroll wheel) work too.

The camera feed never leaves your device — it's only used to sense movement, and nothing is recorded or sent anywhere. You can turn it off any time.
